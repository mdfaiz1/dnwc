Acupuncture is a process which is done by inserting needles at certain point’s body to balance their energy. This boosts wellbeing of person and cures some health problems also.

Conditions it is used for include different kinds of pain, such as headaches, whooping cough ,blood pressure problems.

How Acupuncture Works?

According to traditional Chinese medicine health is the result of a harmonious balance of the corresponding extremes of "yin" and "yang" of the life force which is known as "qi,” and pronounced as "chi." Illness is the consequence of an imbalance of the forces.

Qi is said to pathways, in the human body. These energy flows are accessible via 350 acupuncture points in the body.

By inserting needles into these points with suitable combinations brings the energy flow back into proper balance.

Some of the experts have used neuroscience for explanation of acupuncture. Acupuncture points are visualized at the places where muscles, nerves, and connective tissue can be stimulated. It increases blood flow, which at the same time also activates body's natural painkillers.

Benefits of Acupuncture

  • Safe with No Side Effects

    Acupuncture if performed well with caution, it is safe has no side effects.

  • Goes With Other Treatments

    Acupuncture can be successfully combined with other treatments.

  • Controls Pains

  • Works for Most

    It proves to help patients for whom pain medications are not suitable.
