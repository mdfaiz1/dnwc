Story Behind Founding Arogyadhama

Arogyadhama is one of the oldest organisation founded in the year of 2003 by Dr Y Rudrappa who is one of the senior professional in the system Yoga &Naturopathy in India. Arogyadhama formed to help the society with its unique system of healing the disease with drugless therapy.

Since inception Arogyadhama was constantly working on spreading the awareness about importance of drugless system of medicine through Yoga and Naturopathy by conducting several camps in different parts of Karnataka.

Arogyadhama has conducted multiple awareness programs to educate the society about the system of Yoga &Naturopathy. With the above objective Arogyadhama felt a need to set up Yoga & Naturopathy clinics to provide better facility by physically being on the ground. Therefore the 1st Naturopathy and Yoga clinic was setup in Bangalorein the year 2010. After a good response and results Arogyadhama expanded its operation and opened 2 more new branches to serve the society through drugless system.

Arogyadhama Wellness is now leading healthcare solutions of drugless therapy specialized in Yoga & Naturopathy. Arogyadhama emerged with noble solutions to health care seekers through Preventive, Educative and Curative activities through the principles of holistic practice of medicine, Complimentary and alternate medicine.
