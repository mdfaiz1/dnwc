Story Behind Founding Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre

Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre is one of the oldest organisation founded in the year of 2003 by Dr Y Rudrappa who is one of the senior professional in the system Yoga &Naturopathy in India. Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre formed to help the society with its unique system of healing the disease with drugless therapy.

Since inception Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre was constantly working on spreading the awareness about importance of drugless system of medicine through Yoga and Naturopathy by conducting several camps in different parts of Karnataka.

Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre has conducted multiple awareness programs to educate the society about the system of Yoga &Naturopathy. With the above objective Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre felt a need to set up Yoga & Naturopathy clinics to provide better facility by physically being on the ground. Therefore the 1st Naturopathy and Yoga clinic was setup in Hazaribagin the year 2010. After a good response and results Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre expanded its operation and opened 2 more new branches to serve the society through drugless system.

Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre Wellness is now leading healthcare solutions of drugless therapy specialized in Yoga & Naturopathy. Dwarika Naturopathy & Wellness Centre emerged with noble solutions to health care seekers through Preventive, Educative and Curative activities through the principles of holistic practice of medicine, Complimentary and alternate medicine.
